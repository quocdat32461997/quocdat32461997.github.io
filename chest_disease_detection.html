Transfer Learning for Chest Disease Detection

Sep., 2019 - I and 3 other students joined an AI Medical Hackathon held by Mr. Tarry Singh and Cuare AI. This is to show steps we made during the competition.

Problem: X-ray scans of chest areas have been used to detect cancer and other kinds of diseases within Chest area. However, the diagnosis based on the X-ray scans by humans is time-consuming. Hence, the question is to develop Machine Learning algorithm to quickly identify diseases on X-Ray scans.
